About Halo

Yvete Simoes is a duel licensed Esthetician and Massage Therapist. She has been practicing Massage Therapy for over half a decade. Yvete attended the Elizabeth Grady School of Esthetics and Massage Therapy where she studied and completed anatomy, physiology and pathology of the human body as well as a ray of different techniques to therapeutically manipulate muscular tissue to release tension even from the most deepest muscle layers.

At Halo Massage, we provide customized rehabilitation massage therapy that targets the root cause of their discomfort. We use a variety of techniques such as Deep Tissue, Cupping Therapy, Sports Injury and Orthopedic injury massage to promote healing and restore function. Each treatment plan is designed based on individual needs, with a focus on long-term recovery and injury prevention. Through ongoing support, education, and feedback, we ensure our clients feel heard, empowered, and on the path to recovery.

Halo Massage accepts FSA (Flexible Spending Account) and HSA (Health Savings Account) spending cards.

Cancellation Policy

If you cancel or reschedule your appointment within 24 hours of your scheduled time, a cancellation fee of 50% of your service cost will be charged. If you reschedule or no-show within 4 hours of your appointment, 100% of your service cost will be charged as a cancellation fee.

A credit card deposit is required at the time of booking for all new clients or appointments exceeding 50% of the service cost. This deposit will only be charged in the event of a late cancellation, reschedule, or no-show as outlined above. Please note: deposits are nonrefundable under any circumstances. This policy ensures your time, as well as ours, is respected, as appointment slots fill up quickly.

In return, we promise to:
